About Us

We use project-based fun learning program designed to introduce young children to the world of electronics, microcontrollers, and programming. With the aim of developing their curiosity and creativity, we use hands-on approach to engage children in creating their own projects, thus enabling them to apply their knowledge in a practical way. Through the program, children are exposed to various electronics concepts and gain a basic understanding of programming languages. With the support of skilled mentors, children are guided through the program in a fun and engaging way, building their confidence in the subject matter and developing valuable skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, and teamwork. We believe that project based learning is an exciting and innovative way to inspire and nurture young minds.

Our Team

Manisha Agrawal

Manisha Agrawal is an Electrical Engineer by training and has an extensive experience in developing and managing complex products in missile guidance, telecommunications, and multimedia space. She has about 18 years of experience having worked with organizations such as IIT Mumbai, Tata Elxsi, Philips Semiconductors and with start-ups and small-scale enterprises such as Ncore, Ionic Microsystems and Innoviti Payment Solutions, a company with cutting edge technology in transaction processing in the payment space. She believes that her experience in the industry can help her guide them in practical and hands-on approach towards learning.

Vidya Kodimoole

Vidya Kodimoole is a distinguished educator with an outstanding academic background and over 15 years of experience in the field of education. She holds a Master’s degree in Power Electronics. Her impressive academic performance earned her second rank in the university. Fuelled by her passion for teaching, Vidya worked as lecturer in some of the leading institutions, including NITK Surathkal. Over the last decade she has been teaching engineering students across the globe through online mediums, demonstrating her unwavering commitment to education. In 2017, Vidya’s dedication to teaching expanded beyond engineering as she began imparting STEM education to children aged 10 years and above. She designed courses to inspire young minds to explore and learn. Her efforts have not gone unnoticed as she has successfully guided numerous school children in their projects, equipping them with the necessary skills to thrive in the future. She also has volunteered in a government school to teach English language.
